Music

I started making music in my freshmen year of highschool. I do not know anything about music. It is the one thing I am able to do without obsessing over it and being a perfectionist. It is not meant to be good, it is just meant as a break for me to tell myself that something doesn't have to be perfect to put it out there.


In 2023 I released my first EP and later that year, my first full album, all completely digital.
